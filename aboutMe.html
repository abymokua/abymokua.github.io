Company History

Nova Pioneer is a panafrican network of schools currenty in South Africa and Kenya with the hope of expanding to 100 schools across the continent

CEO, Chinezi Chijioke began his career as a mathematics teacher and later became the head of the African education intiative in McKinsey & co. The core aim of the organisation is to provide quality education at an afordable cost, that prepares and equips the youth with necessary skills to thrive in the African Century

The first campus in Kenya launched in January 2016 and has since grown into 3 campuses, with the fourth launching in January 2019
